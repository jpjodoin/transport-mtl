Schedule Update

The schedule update isn’t made via Android Market. To update your schedule data, you should click on the Menu button on your phone and the select update. Follow the instructions. It’s possible that you can’t perform the update because the server isn’t reachable. Therefore, try the update later in the day. If this persists, please send us email (support@rhatec.net) and we can advise you of the situation.

Schedule Validity

You can see schedule’s validity on the search page of bus stop. We can’t always provide schedule’s validity. In this case, you should check frequently if there is an update.

Notifications

You can add a notification before next passage of bus. To perform that, click on the bus passage time that you are interested in. After that, assign how long before bus passage did you need to be notified. Depending on your phone settings, your phone will vibrate / ring to alert you.

Bookmarks

You can add a bus stop to your bookmarks to easily access it. For that, go to the bus stop schedule, click on the menu button of your phone and after that, add it to your bookmarks. You can access your bookmarks by clicking on the menu button and selecting bookmarks. The next bus passage will be shown for each bus stop.

Start page

It's possible to change the start page of the application. That way, you can choose the bookmarks as a start page. To do that, go in Settings and choose the Start page option.

Settings

Settings of the application can be reached by clicking on the menu button, then on preference. You can also reach this user guide, through the preference main menu.

Data storage

Application’s data is stored in your Sd card under the folder transportmtl. It’s important to not edit or delete this data, because it can crash your application. If you uninstall this application, you can delete this folder to gain space.

Feature improvement and bugs

If you need support with this application or if you want to report bugs, please ensure that you have the last software’s version and the last database. If you get the same behavior, please send us a mail to support@rhatec.net. Please provide as much as details as you can to help us to investigate the problem. Also, our software isn’t perfect. Without your valuable feedback, we can’t fully meet your expectations. With this in mind, assign a bad evaluation to this application without sufficient detail or without having first contacted us is not beneficial. Indeed, we do not have your email address to contact you for more details and thereby improve the application.
